Daniel Tupou, born on June 17, 1991, is a professional rugby league footballer who plays as a winger for the Sydney Roosters in the NRL. He has represented both Tonga and Australia internationally and has played for the City Origin and New South Wales in the State of Origin series, as well as the Prime Minister's XIII. Tupou, of Tongan descent, began his junior rugby league with the Berala Bears and Guildford Owls before joining the Parramatta Eels. He made his NRL debut for the Roosters in 2012 and has since won NRL Grand Finals in 2013, 2018, and 2019. Tupou holds the record as the Sydney Roosters' all-time leading try scorer and continues to be a key player for the club, signing a contract extension until the end of the 2026 season.
