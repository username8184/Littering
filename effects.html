Effects of Littering

Different individuals and sources contribute to the effects of litter. For example, it could be purposeful littering by drivers throwing trash out of windows, by pedestrians leaving trash on the sidewalks or the street, or by individuals attending public events like picnics. The existence and evolution of people, animals, and the natural environment can all be threatened by litter. The main effects are the risk to human health, the potential for fire dangers, the potential for species to be endangered or killed, and the significant harm to rivers. Litter also has an impact on our quality of life and the tourism industry, both directly and indirectly. Because of this careless pollution, removing litter from the environment costs a lot of money that could have been spent on protecting the environment.

Felise Kaufusi, born on May 19, 1992, is a 31-year-old professional rugby league footballer from New Zealand who plays as a second-rower for the Dolphins in the NRL. He previously played for the Melbourne Storm, where he won the NRL Grand Finals in 2017 and 2020, and has represented both Tonga and Australia at the international level, including competing for the Queensland Maroons in the State of Origin series. Kaufusi began his rugby journey in Auckland, moved to Australia at age seven, and played junior football for the Bundaberg Brothers before joining the North Queensland Cowboys' NYC team. He made his NRL debut in 2015 and has since established himself as a key player, known for his athleticism and resilience.

Daniel Tupou, born on June 17, 1991, is a professional rugby league footballer who plays as a winger for the Sydney Roosters in the NRL. He has represented both Tonga and Australia internationally and has played for the City Origin and New South Wales in the State of Origin series, as well as the Prime Minister's XIII. Tupou, of Tongan descent, began his junior rugby league with the Berala Bears and Guildford Owls before joining the Parramatta Eels. He made his NRL debut for the Roosters in 2012 and has since won NRL Grand Finals in 2013, 2018, and 2019. Tupou holds the record as the Sydney Roosters' all-time leading try scorer and continues to be a key player for the club, signing a contract extension until the end of the 2026 season.
