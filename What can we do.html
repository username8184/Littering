What can we do to stop Littering in NZ

As demonstrated by a recent National Litter Audit that found an unbelievable 436% rise in litter since 2019, littering is a major problem in New Zealand. To properly address this problem, comprehensive efforts need to be put into place. Increasing the strictness of current littering rules is one workable option. According to research, people are less likely to litter when they believe they have a high chance of getting caught. Therefore, harsher punishments and more surveillance may discourage potential lawbreakers.
